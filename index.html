Geospatial Data Analysis

Geospatial analysis using qGIS and geopandas was conducted for a Remote Sensing Project evaluating the influence of urban tree canopy on urban heat islands in Miami, Florida. The project resulted in the development of the Heat Vulnerability Index, the creation of maps of the most vulnerable census zones, and the prediction of the outcomes of increased tree canopy coverage in vulnerable areas.
